All Time Best Movies according to Derrick

My Top 3 Movies

Haikyuu: Battle of the Garbage Dump

There isn't enough I can say about this anime movie. It is a part of an anime series called Haikyuu which is about volleyball. Conentrating on live lessons through volleyball and showing incredible character development. You fall in love with the characters and can relate them to your life.

Star Wars: Revenge of the sith

The whole of the Star Wars saga is incredible however this one really cemented itself in the saga as the movie with the best story telling and movie reveals. You get this effect even more if you watch the two inital trilogies in the same manner Goerge Lucas produced the movies. First starting with second trilogy (4-6) then watching (1-3) with 3 being "Revenge of the Sith".

Sonic 3

This one is more recent and is in the list for the emotions it makes you feel and also the pure nostalgia. However, due to the cast and writing it elevates the movie above a normal video game movie. It is thrilling, captivating and has characters you will fall in love with.
